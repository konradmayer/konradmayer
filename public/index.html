Konrad Mayer

DATA SCIENTIST | EDUCATOR | WOOD SCIENTIST | DENDROCHRONOLOGIST

Scientist at BOKU Vienna at the BIOnami group, using scientific computing to analyze hyperspectral data. Former dendrochronologist at the tree-ring research lab with main research interests in the fields of dendroclimatology, -hydrology, as well X-ray densitometry with a focus on intra annual density profiles. Konrad is passionate about scientific programming, particularly in R, data science as well as imaging techniques and is author and maintainer of several R packages (hyperSpec.utils, iadf, trlboku) as well as apps, among them peakfit.bionami.at and oldestwoodenobjects.net.
